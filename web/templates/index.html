BeNotarization

Blockchain Technology is applied to this notarization platform to provide available and reliable notarial service.

 

Applicable Scenario

Bilateral contracts

 

If A and B have made a bilateral contract, our platform can provide notarization for the contract. Each of them can make a promise to the other with the content of the contract and their signatures. Then, these promises will be packed in a block in the blockchain as a transaction. Everyone can look up these promises to ensure the contract is valid, which will be a reliable evidence if anyone contravenes the contract.

Receipts for loans

 

If A has lent something to B, our platform can provide notarization for the loan. B needs to make a promise to A with its signature to verify that it has gotten something from A. Then, this promise will be packed in a block in the blockchain as a transaction. Everyone can look up this promise to ensure it is valid, which will be a reliable evidence if conflict happens.

 

Services promises

 

If B has promised to provide long term services to A like product warranty, our platform can provide notarization for them. B needs to make a promise to A with the content of service and its signature. Then, this promise will be packed in a block in the blockchain as a transaction. Everyone can look up this promise to ensure it is valid, which will be a reliable evidence if conflict happens.

Deposit

 

If A has paid deposit to B for something, our platform can provide notarization for it. B needs to make a promise to A with its signature to verify the deposit and future deliverables. Then, this promise will be packed in a block in the blockchain as a transaction. Everyone can look up this promise to ensure it is valid, which will be a reliable evidence if conflict happens.
